Who Are We?

In a heartwarming journey, two kindergarten best friends, Hriti and Laxita, embarked on a skincare venture inspired by Hriti's father, a seasoned skincare expert. Fueled by their generational knowledge, they transformed their passion into a small business. Hriti's father shared his expertise, guiding the duo in formulating gentle and natural skincare solutions suitable for all ages. From whimsical product names to packaging, the young entrepreneurs infused their products with a touch of childhood joy. Through this endearing collaboration, Hriti and Laxita not only created effective skincare but also cherished a bond that transcended generations, proving that the beauty of friendship can bloom into something extraordinary.
