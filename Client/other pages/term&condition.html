How will my order be delivered to me?

+

Once your order is placed on the website, a confirmation email will be sent to your mail id. The order will be packed, shipped and delivered in 7 working days. All the updates regarding the parcel shall be constantly upload on the given email.

Can I cancel my order?

+

You can cancel the order with full refund if the shipment has not picked the parcel from our destination, any further delay in cancellation will cost you the shipment charges, but the products money shall be refunded 100%.

How do I check the status o my order?

+

The updates regarding order will uploaded on your given mail via the shipping company which will provide its link for tracking your order.
